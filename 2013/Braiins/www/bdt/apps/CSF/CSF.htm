Final

Final should be set once the accounts have been submitted to HMRC and Companies House.

The 'Final' status then prevents any further data change until a 'Start New Year' is performed.

Financial Statements may still be produced and downloaded.

The 'Final' status setting may be removed or reversed by an Administrator.

Criteria

An Entity can be set to Final when:

  • The Data State is OK
  • The Accounts State is 'Final Candidate'
