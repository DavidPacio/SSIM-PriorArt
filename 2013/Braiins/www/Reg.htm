Register with Braiins

Braiins produces iXBRL accounts for online submission to the United Kingdom’s HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC).

Braiins:

  • is intended for use by UK HMRC Agents, normally Accounting Practices.
  • currently only takes input from SAPA (Sage Accounts Production Advanced). Other input source options will be added, but right now, if you are not using SAPA, then Braiins is not for you.
  • applies only to Audit Exempt companies reporting under GAAP. Support for other entity types and IFRS will follow.

To use Braiins you need to register, which you do here.

No Commitment

Registration does not mean commitment. Braiins is a pay as you go service, with no fixed term, and no annual or monthly fees. You can cancel (de-register or delete your Braiins account) at any time. Braiins works via credits. You buy credits as and when you want, with discounts for bulk purchases, and use them to pay for each set of accounts produced. For details see Prices.)

Free to Start

On registering you are gifted 10 credits, which will enable you to produce one set of accounts for submission to HMRC. (This is the Free Trial described on the Free Trial page.) If you want to continue with Braiins to produce more sets of accounts, as we trust you will, you will need to buy more credits, as few or a as many as you decide. Whereas if you conclude that Braiins is not what you are seeking, then just delete your account when logged in to the Braiins Desktop, and that is it.

GUARANTEE:

  • If you are unable to achieve 100% tagging of a set of accounts through Brains, with no manual tagging after the event, the credits charged for those accounts will be refunded.
  • If you find the tagging audit report produced by Braiins to be inadequate, and you are forced to waste time using a manual tagging tool to review what has been iXBRL tagged in a set of accounts, the credits charged for those accounts will be refunded.
  • If any accounts produced via Braiins should be rejected by HMRC as either invalid iXBRL or for non-compliance with HMRC requirements, double the credits charged for those accounts will be refunded.
  • If you stop using Braiins, and you have unused credits in your account, the cost of those credits will be refunded. There is no time limit on this guarantee.
