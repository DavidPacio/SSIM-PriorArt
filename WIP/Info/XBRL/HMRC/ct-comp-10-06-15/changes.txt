SUMMARY OF THE MAIN CHANGES TO THE TAXONOMY
-------------------------------------------

Computation of tax payable
New elements to allow the calculation at up to three rates for each financial year.

Finance Act 2008
New elements to cover:
* First year tax credits
* Annual investment allowances
* Special rate pool

Capital allowances
We have added sections for "other" capital allowances and made some rationalisations in the capital allowances sections.

Adjustment of profit for Schedule D Case I
Schedule A income or loss
Adjustment of trading profit (Case V)
Income from property (Case V)
We have made changes to the sections for trading profits and income that is computed on a similar basis.

Capital gains
We have replaced a plethora of separate computations dependent on when the asset was acquired with a single generic chargeable gains computation.

Analysis of profit and loss account
We have introduced an alternative analysis of the profit and loss account.

General
We have tried to improve the consistency of the naming of elements and removed some redundant elements.
