Delete Entity

Click the row of an Entity to Delete it from Braiins.

  • Deleting an entity removes all data for the entity from Braiins except for Agent transactions involving the entity.
  • The reference used for the entity cannot be used again.
  • Entity deletion cannot be reversed.
  • At least one Entity must remain in your account, even if just one of the demo entities.
  • If you decide to close your Braiins Account all entities are deleted so you need not delete them here first.
  • If you click to select an entity, a further confiirmation step occurs before the entity is deleted.
