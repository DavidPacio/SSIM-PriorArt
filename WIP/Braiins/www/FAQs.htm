Braiins Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q.

I have entered my invoices in the Business Simulator, set up a TARI® and targeted for the year ahead. What more do I need to do?
A. You have done well! However, setting up TARI® and targeting ahead is only the first step on the road to boosting the bottom line. You need to track your invoices and compare them with TARI® day-by-day as well as track and compare the total weekly and accumulatively.

Subscribing for TARI enables you to do that.

Q.

Can't I do that on a spreadsheet without having to subscribe?
A. Spreadsheets are fine for a limited number of invoices, however they lack the flexibility required when it comes to accumulating, extracting, grouping and comparing by product, customer or personnel with TARI® provided by the software.

Q.

How much does TARI Cost?

A. US$27.75 per month for the TARI for Business software supplied via the Jade Subscription service. There is no contract or lock-in. You can cancel at any time. Though once you start seeing how much TARI can do for your business, we don't think you will ever want to be without it.

If you would like direct personal contact for advisory input and back-up from the TARI team, a limited number of Platinum Subscriptions are available. For these, the price depends on individual business needs, and is agreed with you by phone and/or email once you have completed the Platinum Subscription - Expression of Interest form to start the process.

Consulting advice is also available from independent TARI Experts, Accountants or Management Consultants, located via TARI Network. In this case, fees would be negotiated directly between the consultant/advisor and you.

Q.

Is the software for sale?

A.No, subscription only, as above.

Q.

What benefits will TARI® give me?

A.TARI will:
  • Increase productivity, output and hence profitability
  • Focus pricing and planning decisions only on those activities which give your business a competitive edge
  • Save you money and time as you know which transactions are unprofitable
  • Quickly provide clear, relevant, usable information: feedback of results compared with target, and a woods-eye view of overall performance at all times
  • Unite and inspire management and staff to continually improve business performance by understanding how their unique contribution impacts the business
For full information see the Braiins Features and Benefits page.

Q.

What help do you provide with getting us going with the Jade level subscription?
A. TARI for Business comes with an already set-up business that you can "play with" to become familiar with the software before setting up your own business.

As well, the software includes extensive integrated help available at every step.

Then training videos are available online. There are currently 8 of these, two public:
  • TARI Introductory Video
  • TARI Business Simulator Introductory Video
and six training videos for Members (subscribers):
  • Overview Part 1
  • Overview Part 2
  • Creating a New Business File
  • File Setup
  • Using What If
  • Invoice Grouping for Sales

Q.

You say the Jade level subscription is supported with integrated help, email support, and online with traning videos, FAQs, newsletters, blogs etc. What if we come across a bug in the software?
A. If any bugs are found they will be corrected post-haste.

Q.

As a Jade member, what if we need non-software advice about our business. Can we contact you?
A. The low monthly subscription cost of a Jade membership, intended to make TARI accessible to almost any business in the world, does not permit individual consulting to be provided.

However, as indicated in "How much does TARI cost?" above, both software and non-software consulting advice is available to Platinum level members via at an agreed (higher) monthly subscription rate set according to the business and its needs.

Further, consulting advice is available from independent TARI Experts, Accountants or Management Consultants, located via TARI Network.
